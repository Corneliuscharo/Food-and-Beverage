Company Owner's History

Cornelius Charo Majaliwa was born in Pangayambo village, Kilifi County in Kenya, in the year 1997. He attended Pangayambo Primary School before proceeding to Shimo la Tewa Secondary School in Mombasa County for his high school education. After completing his secondary education, Cornelius joined Kirinyaga University, where he pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics and graduated in the year 2022. He is currently working from his home office as a Data Analyst and planning to enroll in a Computing PhD program in Data Science at Boise State University in the United states of America. Apart from his writing profession, Cornelius is also a successful entrepreneur, owning several companies including DATAWISE Analytics Solution Ltd, Smart Investors, and Cornelius Food and Beverages Company.
